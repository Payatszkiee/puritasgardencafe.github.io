ABOUT PURITA'S

DID YOU KNOW?

Purita's Garden Café is a restaurant managed and handled by the five children of the late Mrs. Purita Sagala Perea and the late Mr. Bernardo Perea: Joel Perea, Elrani Perea, Maya Perea Santos, Eric Perea, and Nova Perea. The name of the restaurant itself was basically inspired by the name of their beloved mother and was established on the 23rd of November, year 2019.

The eldest, Mr. Joel Perea, is inclined to both culinary and landscaping practices. It also happened that Mrs. Purita really loved the concept of gardening. These are the primary reasons why they have decided to establish a Garden Café type of restaurant.

For the main staffs who work in unison to provide the best Filipino cuisine and service to the customers: Mr. Joel Perea is the main cook who is responsible for the food served to the customers; Mr. Elrani Perea is the person assigned to the aesthetics and sanitation of the restaurant; Mrs. Maya Perea Santos is the financial manager; Mr. Eric Perea is the one accountable for the annex of the restaurant (restobar); and Ms. Nova Perea is the overall manager of Purita's Garden Café.

Our mission is to serve quality food and service to our customers, given the perfect ambiance coming from the gift of nature. We always prioritize our customers, in terms of safety and satisfaction. Thus, we constantly assure that the cravings of our customers are met in a secure manner.
